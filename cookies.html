Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how GreenScape Gardening Services (“we”, “us”, “our”) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. It informs you about what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding their use. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this Policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) by websites you visit. They help websites remember your actions, preferences, and device information to enhance your user experience. Cookies can be “session” cookies, which are temporary and deleted after you close your browser, or “persistent” cookies, which remain on your device for a set period.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the operation of our website. They enable you to navigate the site and use its features, such as accessing secure areas or maintaining your session. Without these cookies, some services may not function properly.

2. Analytical and Performance Cookies

These cookies collect anonymous information on how visitors use our website, which pages are most frequently visited, and if users encounter error messages. We use this data to improve the website’s functionality and user experience. Examples include Google Analytics cookies.

3. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies remember your preferences and settings to provide enhanced, personalized features. For example, language preferences or login details may be stored to customize your visits.

4. Advertising and Targeting Cookies

These cookies collect information about your browsing habits to deliver relevant advertisements on our website or third-party sites. They help us measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns. We do not use any third-party advertising cookies that collect personal data directly on this site without your consent.

Third-Party Cookies

Some services on our site, such as embedded videos, social media features, or analytics, may place cookies from third-party providers. We do not control these cookies and recommend reviewing third-party privacy policies for more information.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure proper website functionality and security.
  • Analyze how visitors interact with the site and improve navigation.
  • Remember your preferences for a more personalized experience.
  • Support marketing and advertising efforts in compliance with privacy laws.

Your Cookie Choices

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse all cookies, accept only certain types, or delete cookies. However, disabling necessary cookies may prevent essential website features from working properly.
