Privacy Policy

At GreenScape Gardening Services, your privacy is a top priority. We are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your data. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding personal data, your rights, and how you can contact us if you have any concerns or questions.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of personal data when you interact with our website or use our services:

  • Contact Information: such as your name, email address, phone number, and postal address.
  • Account and Service Information: details of the services you inquire about or purchase, preferences, and any correspondence you send to us.
  • Technical Data: such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and browsing behavior collected via cookies or similar tracking technologies.
  • Payment Information: credit/debit card details or other payment data provided when making purchases (processed securely through trusted third parties).

2. How We Collect Information

We collect personal information directly from you when you contact us, fill out forms, request quotes, place orders, or subscribe to newsletters. We also automatically collect certain technical data using cookies and tracking technologies when you visit our website to improve your browsing experience.

3. Use of Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Provide, manage, and deliver our gardening services tailored to your needs.
  • Respond to your inquiries, requests, and support needs promptly.
  • Process payments securely and manage billing.
  • Send you updates, promotional offers, and newsletters (only with your consent).
  • Improve our website, services, and customer experience through analytics.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance functionality and user experience. Cookies allow us to remember your preferences, analyze traffic, and provide personalized content. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings; however, this may affect some website features.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Your personal information may be shared with trusted service providers who assist with payment processing, marketing, website hosting, or customer service, strictly for the purposes outlined above and under confidentiality agreements. In rare cases, we may disclose information to comply with legal requirements or protect our rights.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or misuse. Our staff is trained on data protection principles, and we regularly review security protocols to maintain the highest standards.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access and obtain a copy of your data.
  • The right to rectify inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • The right to request deletion or restriction of data processing.
  • The right to object to certain types of processing.
  • The right to data portability.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below. We will respond promptly in accordance with applicable laws.

8. Retention of Data

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services and website are not directed at children under 16 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe we have collected data from a child inadvertently, please contact us for immediate removal.

10. International Transfers

If you are located outside the country where we operate, your personal data may be transferred to, stored, and processed in our country or other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during such transfers.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in laws, technology, or business practices. The updated policy will be posted on our website with the revision date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
